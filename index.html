About Us

Manthan Foundation is a registered entity working diligently for the last 5 years in the field of literary art, education, and empowerment. We believe in providing a platform to raise the right voices at the right time, fostering meaningful change in society.

Change is possible only through collective efforts, and we actively seek participation and collaboration with like-minded individuals and organizations.

Join us in creating an ecology of change, where every contribution makes the world a better place for future generations.

Here is a tiny glimpse of the work done by Manthan Foundation.

Our Programs

Kalamanthan

Devoted to the love for Hindi literature, our goal is to inspire and support the next generation of writers, poets, and storytellers by bringing together both newcomers and stalwarts.

Aao Kahani Baatein

We believe that emotional intelligence is crucial for personal and societal well-being. Through stories and interactive sessions, we help students develop skills to understand and manage emotions, fostering healthier relationships and communities.

Project Saath

Empowerment of women is at the heart of our foundation. We offer support, resources, and training to help women achieve economic independence, personal growth, and leadership skills.

Beyond Books

A project dedicated to promoting critical thinking, knowledge-sharing, and education beyond traditional learning methods. We foster a love for learning through workshops, book discussions, and creative activities.

Samanta

Focused on gender equality and inclusivity, this program works towards breaking stereotypes and promoting equal opportunities for all, regardless of gender identity and background.

A Little Glimpse of our World

Our aim is to inspire, empower, and create a positive impact in our community through our diverse programs and initiatives. Join us in making a difference.
